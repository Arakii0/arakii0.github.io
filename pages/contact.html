Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you offer in cybersecurity?

I offer a comprehensive range of cybersecurity services including penetration testing, vulnerability assessments, security architecture design, incident response planning, security awareness training, and compliance consulting. Each service can be tailored to meet the specific needs of your organization.

How do you approach a new cybersecurity project?

My approach begins with understanding your business objectives and security requirements. I then conduct a thorough assessment of your current security posture, identify gaps and vulnerabilities, develop a strategic plan to address these issues, implement the necessary controls and solutions, and provide ongoing monitoring and support as needed.

What industries have you worked with?

I have experience working across various industries including financial services, healthcare, retail, technology, manufacturing, and government. Each industry has unique security challenges and compliance requirements, and I bring industry-specific knowledge to address these effectively.

Are you available for remote work or consulting?

Yes, I am available for both remote work and consulting engagements. Many cybersecurity tasks can be performed remotely, and I have the tools and experience to work effectively in a distributed environment. For certain assessments or implementations, on-site work may be beneficial, which can be arranged as needed.

How do you stay current with evolving cybersecurity threats?

I maintain a rigorous approach to continuous learning, including participating in professional communities, attending security conferences, pursuing advanced certifications, monitoring threat intelligence feeds, engaging in hands-on labs and CTF competitions, and contributing to open-source security projects. This ensures my knowledge and skills remain at the cutting edge of cybersecurity.
